Dominance Ratio

An interactive sequence of the Dominance Ratio for the Australian open from 2004 - 2014.

The Dominance Ratio of a player for a match is the ratio of the percentage of points won on an opponent's serve to the percentage of points lost on the player's own serve. Ideally a player would want to win on the opponent's serve and not lose serve in order to stand a good chance of winning. When this trend starts developing, it means that a player wins points on serve and on the opponent's serve and is dominating proceedings in a match.
